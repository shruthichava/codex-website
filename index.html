WHAT IS BLUEPRINT?

WHAT IS
BLUEPRINT?

A student-led community where CAD, Robotics, and Engineering come together through hackathons, CAD competitions, and collaborative workshops.

Through competitive experiences, Blueprint encourages students to develop an interest in STEM by making technical learning more interactive and rewarding. These events allow students to challenge themselves, work with others, and gain hands-on experience while exploring like design, programming, and engineering.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Participants must be students in grades 4-8 (Junior) or 9-12 (Senior).

Do I need experience?+

Not at all! There will be mentors to help you along the way.

How much does it cost?+

The event is completely free!

What should I bring?+

A laptop or computer that can support the software you would like to use.
